A Website FUR your Health

A website or online page that provides access to or connections to other websites is known as a portal. Our developers sought to create a Furtal for their pets that would save information about them, such as immunization records, vitamins they are taking, and details on potential ailments.

ABOUT

Furtal was established in 2023 as a website to ensure your pets' safety from potential diseases. It will record information about your pets and their health, as well as what medications or vitamins they are taking. Admins can also post a schedule for free vaccinations and castrane on the portal, and the notification will be uploaded when the date of the schedule approaches.
